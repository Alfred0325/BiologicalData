  • Hello! 

If you'd like a demonstration of the website's functions, please see the video below:

  1. Sign up using a current email. You will be sent a confirmation email.
  2. After you have confirmed your email address, sign in to the website.
  3. Click the Transcribe button on the homepage.
  4. Upload the picture you would like to transcribe.
  5. If you would like, you can click the "Swap image/text location" to swap the sides of the image and transcription box.
  6. If you would like to automatically transcribe the picture, click the "Translate text" button. Please keep in mind that the automatic transcription will work best on images in which the text is regular and strongly contrasted from the background. The official Tesseract OCR documentation has some more information about how to improve the quality of the output, if the automatic transcription is performing poorly for you.
  7. You can also manually transcribe the image in the text box, or edit the automatic transcription result.
  8. Once you are happy with the transcription, you can optionally give the transcription an appropriate title, then click "Submit". This will save your transcription.
  9. If you would like to make any edits to your or other people's previous transcriptions, you can click the "Select" button on the homepage. This will direct you to a page showing all of the previously uploaded pictures and corresponding transcriptions.
  10. If you make an edit to an existing transcription, you will have the choice of overwriting the existing transcription or creating a new transcription when you submit your edits.
